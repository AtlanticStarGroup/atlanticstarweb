FAQ

What exactly do you manage?

We manage the process, not just the logistics. Planning, coordination, access, timing, vendors, on-site supervision, and follow-up. We do everything possible to protect you from unexpected problems.

How do you handle commercial projects?

We approach commercial projects as controlled operations. We plan the sequence, coordinate all parties, manage access and compliance, and supervise execution.

Do you replace movers?

We work with trusted movers and vendors, managing the process around them to ensure timing, access, and decisions are handled correctly.

Do you handle international relocations or overseas shipments?

Yes — as coordination and oversight. We manage the process around international shipping: documentation coordination, timelines, customs-related requirements, and coordination between local and overseas vendors. This helps keep international projects organized.

Do you work with general freight or cargo shipments?

Yes — when freight is part of a larger residential or commercial project. We coordinate general freight or cargo movements with carriers, access, timing, and on-site handling as part of the overall plan.

Do you provide movers or trucks?

No. We manage the project and coordinate vendors (yours or recommended) to keep execution controlled.

What do you need from me to start?

From/to addresses, target dates, building details, and any constraints. Then we take over coordination.

Do you work with building management and COIs?

Yes. We coordinate COIs, elevator bookings, loading zones, access windows, and compliance requirements.

When should I contact you?

Ideally 2–4 weeks before the move. If the timeline is tighter, we’ll assess feasibility quickly.

How does pricing work?

Project-based or hourly depending on scope. Start with a Free Cost Check and we’ll provide a clear estimate.
